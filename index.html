CATECOIN

Catecoin is a cat-themed meme coin that is designed to offer the holder a host of benefits that other meme coins don't have.

Catecoin is currently the only memecoin with multiple utilities. One of Catecoin's top priorities is the security and convenience that their Catecoin wallet provides.

Investors can earn passively through Catecoin's "Hold to Earn" and "Staking and Earn" programs. With reflection embedded in Cates contract, a percentage of the trading transaction will be given to its holders. While long-term investors can enjoy 15% APY by staking their tokens.

Catecoin also offers a "Play to Earn" game named Rise of Cats where gamer can enjoy their time playing while earning multiple rewards.
